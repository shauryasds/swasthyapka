Healing at the Root

At Dr. Richa's Wellness, we focus on healing at the root — whether it's lifestyle disorders, chronic pain, or emotional stress. Below are some of the most common conditions we help reverse and manage:

Lifestyle & Metabolic Disorders

Managing diabetes, thyroid, obesity, PCOD/PCOS, and high blood pressure with root-cause healing and lifestyle changes.

Pain & Mobility Issues

Holistic recovery for arthritis, sciatica, slip disc, migraines, frozen shoulder, and joint stiffness through integrative care.
